What is Crypto Currency?

Crypto-Currency is a digital [Or] virtual currency designed to work as a medium of exchange through a computer network without any central authority, such as a government or bank, maintain it.

What is Blockchain?

Blockchain is a system of recording information in a way that makes it difficult or impossible to change, hack, or cheat the system.

What is Token in Crypto Currency?

A coin usually is native to a blockchain and is used to trade currency and store value. A token is pretty similar, but it tends to use another coin's blockchain. Consider, for example, Ethereum, a blockchain. Its native coin is Ether. However, several other tokens like BAT and HEAL operate on this blockchain.

What is Binance Smart Chain?

The Binance Smart Chain is a smart contract-enabled blockchain network created with the goal of creating a decentralized financial (DeFi) ecosystem. The Binance Smart Chain operates using a Proof-of-Authority consensus mechanism.
